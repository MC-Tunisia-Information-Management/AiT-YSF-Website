Description

With the arising demand for youth entrepreneurship and innovation to bring solutions to solve multiple problems that our society face nowadays, Maghreb countries started encouraging young leaders to create their own startups, AIESEC will bring its professional aspect and expertise to establish a project in which it invites young innovators to put their creative ideas into practice, scale up their projects, Learn for experts and create a network.

Goal

Empower and catalyze the next generation of innovators in collaboration with AIESEC, fostering youth entrepreneurship by providing a platform for young leaders to transform creative ideas into impactful startups, scale their projects, learn from experts, and cultivate a robust network for sustainable success.

SDGs

SDGs are Sustainable Development Goals that we will work on through our event, specifically:

  • Sustainable Development Goal 8 : Economic growth
