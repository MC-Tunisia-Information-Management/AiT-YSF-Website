YouthSpeak Carthage

Description

WELCOME to the genesis of a brighter Tunisia!, AIESEC CARTHAGE’s groundbreaking Youth Speak , a hackathon where innovation meets social impact!.

Our hackathon is uniquely focused on addressing some of the most pressing challenges facing our community related to SDG’s, specifically “Economic Growth”, “Good Health and Well-being” and finally “Industry Innovation and Infrastructure”. Throughout this dynamic event, you will have the opportunity to harness your creativity and technical skills to develop innovative solutions to help make our beloved Tunisia a better place, solutions that will hack to a better tomorrow. And no! This will not stop locally, or even nationally, our national Youth speak winner will be proudly representing Tunisia regionally against Maghreb and Algeria!

Whether you're a passionate coder, or an enthusiastic problem-solver, this is your chance to be part of the solution, a place where you will be guided by mentors and fueled by love for our country to make a tangible impact.
